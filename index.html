FAQs – Good Option (As‑Is)


  

    Can I upgrade the glass, paint, or hardware on the Good option?
    

      No. The Good series is an as‑is product. To keep costs low and lead times short, glass, paint, and hardware are fixed. Price is determined only by window size and type.
    

  


  

    Why doesn’t the Good option allow upgrades?
    

      Good is designed for homeowners who want the most affordable package. By locking specs (standard glass, finish, hardware), we streamline production and pass the savings to you. If you need customization, choose Better or Best.
    

  


  

    What if I select an upgrade by mistake?
    

      Upgrade boxes are disabled when you pick Good, so you’ll never be charged for options that aren’t available. If you want paint colors, hardware finishes, or glass upgrades, switch to Better or Best.
